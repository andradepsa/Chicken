❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 Why based on Bitcoin and not Pi Network?

Proven architecture. Bitcoin has over 15 years of flawless operation. We used its robust architecture as the foundation for PiCoin Extended, ensuring maximum reliability.

🔄 How do cycles renew automatically?

Calendar-based. The system automatically checks the current year and determines which cycle is active. No manual intervention or updates are required.

🛡️ Does this affect PiCoin's security?

Zero negative impact. Maintains 100% of Bitcoin's security. Modifications are only in the emission layer, not in consensus algorithms.

⏰ Why 70 cycles and 140 years?

Millennial planning. 70 × 140 years = 9,800 years of guaranteed operation. Enough time for unimaginable technological and social advancements.

📅 When exactly will the system end?

Year 11,825. Starting in 2025 and lasting 9,800 years total, the system will complete its final cycle in the year 11,825.
